This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to collect Public Transport travel data for use in the preparation of trip plans for Travellers.<br/>(2) The ability to collect the data as it arrives from functionality in the Manage Public Transport Operations Functional Area.<br/>(3) The ability to load the data that has been collected into the store of PT Trip Planning Data and to manage all of the data in that store.<br/>
