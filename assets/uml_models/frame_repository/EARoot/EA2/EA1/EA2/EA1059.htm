Appears In: Functional Tree of Area F2 Provide Safety and Emergency Facilities, DFD F2.2 Manage Stolen Vehicle Reporting
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) An appropriate HMI to communicate to the Emergency Services Operator the notification that a Vehicle has been stolen has been received.<br/>(2) The ability for the HMI to provide some form of alarm indication to draw the attention of the Emergency Operator to the fact that a Vehicle has been stolen.<br/>(3) An appropriate HMI to communicate to the Emergency Services Operator the notification that the stolen notification for a Vehicle has been cancelled, with again some form of alarm indication to draw the attention to the new status.<br/>
