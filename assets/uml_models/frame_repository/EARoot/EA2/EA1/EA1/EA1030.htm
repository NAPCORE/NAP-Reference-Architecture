This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) A HMI that provides the Traveller with the ability to credit their Electronic Payment account.<br/>(2) The HMI shall also include the ability to display to the Traveller the different contracts available to that person, read the information related to the desired transaction.<br/>(3) In addition to the HMI, the ability to check the credit rating of the source account and if credit worthy, credit the Electronic Payment account, plus notify this to the Financial Clearinghouse terminator, and to the store of Transaction Data.<br/>(4) The ability to also check the amount that is being credited to an account previously included in the black list, and if the new balance is sufficient, send a message to the  Credit Control  Function to remove this account from the black-list.<br/>
