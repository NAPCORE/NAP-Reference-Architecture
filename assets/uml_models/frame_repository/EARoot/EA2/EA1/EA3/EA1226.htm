This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to detect that a Vehicle is in a car park space.<br/>(2) The ability to collect this data as raw input by sensors that are capable of detecting the presence of a Vehicle and determining its type, e.g. car, bicycle, Heavy Goods Vehicle (HGV), Public Transport Vehicle. <br/>(3) The ability to send the data about the detected Vehicle to the functionality that calculates the car park spaces occupancies to that it can be determined if a vehicle has exceeded the time it is allowed to occupy a particular space.<br/>
