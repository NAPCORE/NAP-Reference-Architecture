Appears In: DFD F3.1.1.5 Provide Urban Traffic Management Facilities, Functional Tree of F3.1.1 Provide Traffic Control
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to provide management of the lanes on roads in the urban network.<br/>(2) The ability to enable the management of the lanes so that the most efficient use can be made of the road space available in the urban road network.<br/>(3) The ability to enable the use of lanes to be changed in a way that is safe for vehicle operation and that causes the minimum disruption to all forms of urban road traffic.<br/>(4) The ability to send commands that alter the use of lanes to the functionality that is responsible for the output of messages to Drivers, both at the roadside and in the Vehicle.<br/>
