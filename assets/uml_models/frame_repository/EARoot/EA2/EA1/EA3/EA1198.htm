This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to use data collected and stored by other functionality to predict the environmental conditions that will occur in and around the road network managed by the System.<br/>(2) The ability to use this collected and stored data with one or more algorithms and static data provided by the Road Network Operator to predict the environmental conditions that will be experienced by Travellers and/or Drivers.<br/>(3) The ability to send the data providing the predicted environmental conditions for storage by other functionality in the System.<br/>
