It consists of input from the Traveller from which data objects are created that provide requests and emergency notifications provided by a Traveller who wishes to use or is already using PT Services and are for use by functionality in the Manage Public Transport Operations Functional Area.  In this instance the Traveller may also be a Pre-Trip Traveller (waiting at a PT stop), or a Vehicle Sharer.<br/>
