It contains data objects that provide information about predicted unusual traffic conditions within the total road network predicted by the results from traffic simulations and is for use in the monitoring of a trip being implemented by a Driver using an In-vehicle device. The causes of these conditions may include such things as expected road works and road blocks. In both cases the predicted travel times may exceed normal times.<br/>
