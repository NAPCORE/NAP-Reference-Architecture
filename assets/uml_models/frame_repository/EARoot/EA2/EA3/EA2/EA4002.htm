It contains outputs to the Electronic Payments Operator that have been created from data objects provided by functionality in the Provide Electronic Payment Facilities Functional Area and includes information about the contracts established with the Operator during a period specified in the request.  The information objects include:<br/>- operator ID,<br/>- number of contracts for each service ID,<br/>- location of these contracts,<br/>- dates of contracts,<br/>- modes of payment chosen,<br/>- amount of contracts,<br/>- other valuable information (depending on the service nature : for example : quality of service required, duration of contract, …).<br/>
