It contains data objects that provide the identity of the Driver that has just violated the traffic rules in the inter-urban road network.  The data flow is sent after analysis of a violation notification detected and sent by functionality in the Manage Traffic Functional Area.  It contains the following data objects:<br/>- violation notification reference,<br/>- location of violation,<br/>- vehicle ID,<br/>- driver ID.<br/>
