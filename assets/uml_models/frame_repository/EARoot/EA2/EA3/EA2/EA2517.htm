It contains data objects that provide the expected time of arrival (eta) at the interface with another mode of transport of the Freight Vehicle carrying the Freight Transport Load Item(s) according to the Freight Transport Execution Plan that is being implemented to move them.  The data objects shall include but not be limited to the following:<br/> - date,<br/> - time,<br/> - Freight Transport Execution Plan identity,<br/> - required other mode of transport,<br/> - location of transfer to other mode,<br/> - number of Freight Transport Load Items being moved through the implementation of the Plan,<br/> - expected time of arrival (eta),<br/> - further road transport needed,<br/> - location of move back to road transport,<br/> - identity of required Freight Vehicle.<br/>
