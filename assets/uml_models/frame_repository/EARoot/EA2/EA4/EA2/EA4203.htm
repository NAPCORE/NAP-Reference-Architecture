This Terminator shall represent an organisation taking the necessary measures or actions to achieve compliance with laws, rules and regulations that are relevant to road traffic and the movement of freight. Functionality in some parts of the system shall be able to input data objects containing details of any violation of laws, rules and regulations relevant to road traffic and the movement of freight, plus information about required customs clearances and freight vehicle fleet registrations to this Terminator.  In response this Terminator is expected to provide the functionality with data objects containing confirmation of acceptance to the inputs and details of the resulting action that is being taken.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: lea<br/>
