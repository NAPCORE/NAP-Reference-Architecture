The current data type involves static information for identifying a safe and secure truck parking area and providing insight into its attributes. It encompasses the name of the parking area, its specific location, the name of the road(s) (one or more as applies) through a which the parking area is accessible, the name of the exit to be taken to approach the parking area, the distance between the exit to be taken and the entry point of the parking area, the capacity of the parking area (i.e., number of parking places), the time dependent cost of parking, as well as the applicable currency. Moreover, the current data type involves static information about the party operating a truck parking area, including its name and available contact means (e.g., e-mail, telephone).<br/>
