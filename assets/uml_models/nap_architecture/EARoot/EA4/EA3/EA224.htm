To operate objectively and independently from the NO and CPs.<br/><br/><b>Tasks and responsibilities: </b><br/>Randomly and regularly check the compliance of CPs and the NO with the Delegated Regulations (data and metadata are compliant and processes of sharing the data and reporting are compliant with the rules set up by Delegated Regulations). Identify and contact entities obliged to publish (meta)data to the NAP.<br/>
