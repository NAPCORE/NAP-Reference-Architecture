The National Body has the task to provide information to private and public stakeholders, organization, interested persons and answer any other related information request.<br/><br/>Stakeholder:<br/>Stakeholder as main focus group can be informed about their tasks and obligations based on the ITS directive 2010/40/EU and their delegated regulations which includes deadlines, of handing in self declarations for self assessments and random inspections. This information process also includes the offering of direct information meetings and other support activities.  <br/><br/>General information:<br/>The National Body should provide information about acting organizations, the geographical allocation of National Bodies, about National Body obligations, contact details, communication channels (website, e-Mail, hotline, etc) , information about the delegated regulations and regarded deadlines.<br/><br/>For Example: provide a Website with all necessary informations...<br/><br/>
