It contains output to the Multi-Modal Travel Information Provider Terminator which is part of the External Services Providers Terminator that have been created from data objects provided by functionality in the Provide Electronic Payment Facilities Functional Area and includes information about the transactions performed for the benefit of the Multi-Modal Travel Information Provider during a period specified.  The information objects include:<br/>- service provider ID,<br/>- services ID,<br/>- parameters chosen by the users for these services,<br/>- corresponding amount of transactions,<br/>- location of service utilisation,<br/>- revenue generated by services managed by other information providers (revenue sharing),<br/>- revenue distributed to other information providers (revenue sharing)<br/>- total amount credited on each account.<br/>
