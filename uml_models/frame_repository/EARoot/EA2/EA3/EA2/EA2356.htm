It consists of input from the Traveller from which data objects are created that provide details that the Traveller's needs to provide to determine the contract they want to establish with the operator or information provider and are for use by functionality in the Provide Electronic Payment Facilities Functional Area. The inputs will provide: user ID, vehicle ID (if needed to provide the Service), Service ID, parameters defining what is expects of the Service, when the Service will need to be provided, operator or information provider ID, mode of payment and Electronic Payment (EP) account number.<br/>
