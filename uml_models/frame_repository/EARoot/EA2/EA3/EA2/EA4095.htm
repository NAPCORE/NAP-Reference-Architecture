It contains outputs to the Traveller that have been created from data objects provided by functionality in the Provide Electronic Payment Facilities Functional Area and includes a warning that one of their Electronic Payment (EP) accounts is overdrawn, i.e. has no money for the requested payment.  The data objects include the following elements:<br/>- date<br/>- account ID<br/>- user ID<br/>- reference of last transaction<br/>- balance before transaction<br/>- balance after transaction<br/>- minimal amount to credit for account re-use<br/>
