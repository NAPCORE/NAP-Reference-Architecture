It contains output to the Driver that have been created from data objects provided by functionality in the Manage Traffic Functional Area and includes dynamic warnings, which may be about such things as traffic conditions, queues and their rates of propagation,  speed limits, lane commands, road signs and general road information, plus warnings of the need to dip headlights, lane changing, safety behaviour, collision and emergency brake manoeuvres .<br/>
