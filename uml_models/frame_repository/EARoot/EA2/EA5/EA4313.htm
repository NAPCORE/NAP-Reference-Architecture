This Data Store shall be used within the Manage Public Transport Operations Functional Area.  It shall contain information objects that provide the complete description of the schedule PT services.<br/><br/>The information objects in the Store for each service shall contain the following data objects:<br/>(1) ID (number),<br/>(2) Name (characters),<br/>(3) Route (data for a digital roadmap using a standard format, e.g. GDF plus characters and/or numbers, e.g. GPS/Galileo data),<br/>(4) Type of PT Vehicle preferred (characters),<br/>(5) Type of PT Driver preferred (characters),<br/>(6) Type of day on which service operates (characters),<br/>    (6a) Timetable (time in hours and minutes),<br/>    (6b) Frequency (numbers defining hours and minutes),<br/>(7) Variations to the service (characters).<br/><br/>A separate information object shall be provided in the Store for each type of day on which the service operates, e.g. weekday (Monday to Friday), weekend (Saturday and/or Sunday), religious holidays (including Sundays) and other public holidays.  The "variations to the service" shall be used to indicate different service operations that can be applied due to schools being on holiday, factories shut down due to holidays and other regular events that occur during specific time periods on certain types of day.<br/>
