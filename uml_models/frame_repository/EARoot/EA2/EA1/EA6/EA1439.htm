This High-level Function shall provide facilities that enable information to be provided to Travellers, either at the roadside, or by other means.  Information about traffic conditions shall be provided at the roadside, but other forms of information, e.g. about Points of Interest, shall be available to Travellers through other mechanisms, e.g. a nomadic device.<br/>
