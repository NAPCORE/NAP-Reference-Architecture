This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to manage and co-ordinate the use of PT Vehicles that are not currently in use.<br/>(2) The ability to use these additional PT Vehicles as substitutes for faulty vehicles, or to re-enforce those on a particular service, either due to high Passenger loadings or delays.<br/>
