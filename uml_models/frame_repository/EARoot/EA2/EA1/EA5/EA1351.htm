This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to record data that is related to Vehicle safety and/or for understanding accidents.<br/>(2) The data shall include but not be limited to Driver status/behaviour and on vehicle status/dynamic performance from other functionality.<br/>(3) The ability to receive Driver identity data from other functionality.<br/>(4) The ability to collect traffic data through its own sensors.<br/>
