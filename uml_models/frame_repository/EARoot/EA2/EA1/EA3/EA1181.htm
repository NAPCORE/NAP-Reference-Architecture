This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to use a road network model and traffic data to provide predictions of traffic conditions for the road network.<br/>(2) It shall be possible for the current and historical traffic data plus road network data obtained on request from the functionality managing the Traffic Simulation Data to be used to provide the predictions.<br/>(3) The ability to use this data as input to a simulation engine that will predict what the traffic conditions shall be like as a result of various traffic management strategies that are provided by the Transport Planner, or have been used in the past in all or part of the road network.<br/>(4) The ability for these predictions to include the need for car park spaces and shall be produced at the request of the Transport Planner or at regular intervals that the Planner may specify.<br/>(5) When completed the ability to send the predicted traffic conditions and associated strategies to the functionality managing the store of Traffic Simulation Data.<br/>
