This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to collect data about weather conditions that are relevant to the operation of the road network managed by the System.<br/>(2) The ability for some or all of the data to come from Weather Systems or to be detected using sensors within the road network.<br/>(3) The ability to forward the collected data to other functionality for storage.<br/>
