This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to create short and medium term predictions of urban traffic data.<br/>(2) The ability to create the predictions of short and medium term urban traffic data using algorithms that may be different in content and scope.<br/>(3) The ability to request and use current urban traffic data as the starting point for the predictions of short and medium term urban traffic data.<br/>(4) The ability to repeat the creation of the predicted short and medium term urban traffic data at (frequent?) periodic intervals.<br/><br/><br/>
