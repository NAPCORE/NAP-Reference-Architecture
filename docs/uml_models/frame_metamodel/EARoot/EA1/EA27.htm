The ITS service describes the idea and functional elements of the ITS service as well as framework conditions.<br/>The ITS Service is an architectural artifact that describes a value-added service provided by ITS actors in the field of transport, traffic, and mobility for end-users. The added value is created by combining technologies, organizations, processes, and people. The service can be delivered by a single actor or in cooperation with others, and it creates added value that may consist of individual benefits for mobile travelers, collective benefits for end-user groups or society, or a mixture of both. <br/>
