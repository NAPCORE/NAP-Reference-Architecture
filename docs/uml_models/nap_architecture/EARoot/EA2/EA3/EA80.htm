The National Access Point of a Member State has specific tasks related to the Delegated Regulations of the ITS Directive 2010/40/EU, which are requested by the European Commission.<br/>The main task is to provide a non-discriminatory central access information to data listed in Delegated Regulations.<br/><br/><ul>
<li>NAP features shall be language independent (English + local language)</li><li>NAP supports the discovery of metadata and data samples without user registration</li><li>NAP supports data exchange based on standards</li><li>NAP fosters EU wide interoperable ITS services for travelers</li><li>NAP makes data available at standard interfaces and in machine readable formats</li><li>NAP data content is extendable to all ITS-directive domains</li><li>NAP does not infringe the rights of the data owner for the use of data but can support the exchange (e.g. by a standardized license, agreed terms and conditions, technical support with a machine-readable interface definition.)</li></ul><p/>
