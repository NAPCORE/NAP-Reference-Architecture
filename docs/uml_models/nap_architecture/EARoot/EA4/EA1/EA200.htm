Public and private organizations that are using static or dynamic traffic and travel information or any other relevant content, made accessible, under the Delegated Regulations obligations, via NAP, to create multimodal or traffic and travel or mobility information or were provided by the terms and conditions using the data for other related purposes. They are one or a group of actors who get added value by using data promoted by NAP.<br/><br/><b>NOTE1</b>: We do not create objects for each Content Consumer actor type.<br/><b>NOTE2: </b>Content Consumer includes the Data User term as it is specified in MMTIS and RTTI regulations.<br/><br/>Content Consumer actor represents and fulfils the interests of the Content Consumer Stakeholder.  <br/><br/><b>Represented by:</b><br/><ul>
<li>road authorities</li><li>public or private road operator/s</li><li>traffic managers</li><li>tolling operators</li><li>service providers*</li><li>broadcasters dedicated to traffic information</li><li>transport authorities,</li><li>transport operators,</li><li>travel information service providers,</li><li>digital map producers,</li><li>transport on-demand service providers</li><li>infrastructure managers</li><li>data users, any public or private entity or any other entity using data listed in the Delegated Regulations</li></ul><p/>
