Any legal person, data subject, or public or private entity/organization who has the right to grant access to or to share the data under its control. The subject in question shall hold the data that are required to be made accessible via NAP by or more of the Delegated Regulations.<br/><br/><b>NOTE1</b>: We do not create objects for each Content Provider actor type.<br/><b>NOTE2: </b>Content Provider includes the Data Holder term as it is specified in MMTIS and RTTI regulations.<br/><br/>Content Provider actor represents and fulfills the interests of the Content Provider Stakeholders. <br/><br/><b>Represented by:</b><br/><br/>For the RTTI (<font color="#333333">#lt;ins#gt;2015/962#lt;/ins#gt;</font>#lt;ins#gt; #lt;/ins#gt;<font color="#333333">#lt;ins#gt;repealed from 1 January 2025#lt;/ins#gt;</font>#lt;ins#gt; by #lt;/ins#gt;2022/670) concerning:<br/><ul>
<li><b>data on infrastructure</b>: road authorities, public or private road operator/s, tolling operators, recharging and refuelling-related stakeholders</li><li><b>data on regulations and restrictions</b>: road authorities, public or private road operator/s, tolling operators</li><li><b>data on the state of the network</b>: road authorities, public or private road operator/s, holders of in-vehicle generated data, service providers*</li><li><b>data on the real-time use of the network</b>: road authorities, public or private road operator/s, holders of in-vehicle generated data, service providers*, recharging and refuelling-related stakeholders</li></ul>
<br/>* ‘service provider’ means any public or private provider of a real-time traffic information service, excluding a mere conveyer of data to data users;<br/><br/>For the SRTI (886/2013) concerning:<br/><ul>
<li><b>road safety-related traffic data</b>: Service providers, public and/or private road operators, broadcasters dedicated to traffic information, and automotive industries (as service providers when processing vehicle data) [886/2013, art. 7]</li></ul>
<br/>For the SSTP (885/2013) concerning:<br/><ul>
<li><b>static and dynamic information related to safe and secure parking</b>: public or private parking operators and service providers (any public or private body that provides the information service to users) [885/2013 art. 5]</li></ul>
<br/>For the AFIR (2023/1804) concerning:<br/><ul>
<li><b>static and dynamic information related to alternative fuel infrastructure</b>: operators of publicly accessible recharging points and refuelling points for alternative fuels, or, under the arrangements between them, the owners</li></ul>
<br/>For the MMTIS (<font color="#333333">#lt;ins#gt;2017/1926#lt;/ins#gt;</font>#lt;ins#gt; amended by #lt;/ins#gt;2024/490) concerning:<br/><ul>
<li><b>static, historic and observed travel and traffic data</b>: transport authorities, transport operators, infrastructure managers, transport on demand service providers</li><li><b>dynamic travel and traffic data</b>: see above</li><li><b>linking travel information services</b>: travel information service provider</li></ul><p/>
