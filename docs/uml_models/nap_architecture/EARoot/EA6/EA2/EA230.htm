<b>Parties</b>: European Commission (EC) and Member State (MS).<br/><b>Objective</b>: To set up obligations of the MS towards the EC and of the national stakeholders.<br/><b>Form</b>: the ITS Directive and Delegated Regulations <br/><b>Obligations:</b><br/><ul>
<li>EC to MS: ---</li> <li>MS to EC:<ul>
<li>To implement of EU Directives into a natinal law, that reaches the goals set in the ITS Directive, and direct execution of EU Regulations and Delegated Acts including an enforcement process.</li> <li>To periodically report of the progress on the achievement of the goals established in the ITS Directive (and its delegated regulations). </li> <li>To establish the NAP Operator (NO), in the national law; either delegate its establishment to a responsible ministry or directly state the party responsible for NAP operation.</li> <li>To establish the National Body / Competent Authority (NB/CA) in the national law; either delegate its establishment to a responsible ministry or directly state the party responsible for NB/CA operation; create a legal framework for its operation.</li> <li>To ensure compliance with the obligations laid down in EU and national legislation. </li> <li>To establish a legal obligation in national law for ITS service providers to use only ITS components that comply with the specifications set out in EU delegated acts and to provide ITS services in a manner consistent with those specifications</li></ul>
</li><br/></ul>
<br/><b>Reference to the EU law: </b><br/>The obligations of the MS are related to the following European laws:<br/><br/><ul>
<li>2010/40/EU (ITS Directive)</li> <li>886/2013 (SRTI)</li> <li>885/2013 (SSTP)</li> <li>2015/962 (RTTI valid until 31. December 2024)</li> <li>2017/1926 (MMTIS)</li> <li>2022/670 (RTTI in force from 1. January 2025)</li> <li>2023/2661 (ITS Directive amendment)</li> <li>2024/490 (MMTIS amendment)</li> <li>2023/1804 (AFIR)</li></ul>
<br/><b>Selected references to the law:</b><br/><br/><ul>
<li>Each Member State <u>shall set up a National Access Point</u> [2015/962 art. 3, 2017/1926 art. 3, 2022/670 art. 3].</li></ul>
