<b>Parties</b>:  Content Provider (CP) and Content Consumer (CC).<br/><b>Objective</b>:  To set up terms under which the content can be used, and obligations of the CP in terms of quality and accessibility, that are not dealt with by the law.<br/><b>Form</b>: Contract, terms of conditions, license.<br/><b>Obligations:</b><br/><ul>
<li>CP to CC:<ul>
<li>To provide up-to-date and accurate data with a desired level of quality, in the prescribed format.</li></ul>
</li><br/> <li>CC to CP:<ul>
<li>To use the data according to the granted license by the CP (reuse, resell, change, modify).</li></ul>
</li><br/></ul><p/>
