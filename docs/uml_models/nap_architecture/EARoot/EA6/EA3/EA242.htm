<b>IMPORTANT</b>: Content Provider includes the Data Holder term as it is specified in MMTIS and RTTI regulation.<br/><br/><b>Key issue:</b><br/>Publish metadata (and in certain cases data) at the NAP. Provide self-declaration and assistance in case of compliance assessment. Provide data in the prescribed format and with the desired level of quality.<br/><br/><b>Tasks and responsibilities: </b><br/>Publish content they generate based on requirements from the Delegated Regulations under the FRAND conditions. In cases of safety-related traffic information publish content for free.  Be of assistance to the NB/CA when performing a compliance assessment and correct any issues found promptly. Publish data with a desired level of quality in a timely fashion and in a prescribed format. <br/>
