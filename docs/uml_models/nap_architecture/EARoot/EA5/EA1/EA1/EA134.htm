This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities: <br/><br/>(1) The ability to receive and process support requests from any existing or potential NAP Content Provider or NAP Content Consumer. <br/>(2) The ability to respond to user support requests adequately at least in a form of first-level support. <br/>(3) The ability to consult other NAP roles for issues which cannot be responded with the own knowledge. <br/>(4) The ability to manage issues and log the issue resolution for later use by NAP Users or by an Auditing entity <br/><br/>NOTE: this could be done via a ticketing system <br/><br/>=========== <br/><br/>TODO: <br/><br/>- managing and ticketing call for the data store an Issue data store shall be created <br/>- the Auditing entity, i.e. NB is not mentioned anywhere, they shall be created as new terminator in NAP Actors <br/>- NAP Roles are mentioned in the text of the function, it must be clear who this is. <br/>- IMPORTANT: reduce number of dataflows by just addressing all NAP users at once by their higher-level terminator <br/>- IMPORTANT: who is the NAP User?
