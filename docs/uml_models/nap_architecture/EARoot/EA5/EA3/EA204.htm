Enterprise in the European Union generating static or dynamic traffic and travel information content that is explicitly mentioned as an obliged party within one of the Delegated Regulations.<br/><br/><b>Key issue:</b><br/>Publish metadata (and in certain cases data) at the NAP. Provide self-declaration and full assistance in case of compliance assessment.<br/><br/><b>Tasks and responsibilities: </b><br/>Publish content they generate based on requirements from the delegated regulations under the FRAND conditions. In cases of safety-related traffic information publish content for free. Respond to NOs query and publish metadata at NAP. Be of assistance to the NB when performing a compliance assessment and correct any issues found promptly. Publish data with a desired level of quality in a timely fashion and in a prescribed format. <br/>The responsibility is given by being a named entity type and holding named data types.<br/><br/><b>Represented by:</b><br/><ul>
<li>Public and/or private road operators and/or service providers [886/2013, article 7]</li><li>Public or private parking operators and service providers [885/2013 article 5]</li><li>Road authorities and road operators, in cooperation with digital map producers and service providers (any public or private provider of a real-time traffic information service, excluding a mere conveyer of information, to users and end-users). [2015/962 article 3]</li></ul><p/>
