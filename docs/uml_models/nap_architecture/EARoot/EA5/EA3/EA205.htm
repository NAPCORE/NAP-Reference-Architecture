Independent entity responsible for publishing information about content generated by content producers under the Delegated Regulations obligations, to any interested party without prejudice. <br/><br/><b>Key issue:</b><br/>Publish information about Content Producers (CP) and their content in a transparent and timely manner. Provide free-to-all, barrier-free access to information collected by NO.<br/><br/><b>Tasks and responsibilities: </b><br/>Publish information about the responsibilities of the Content Providers, and the purpose of the NAP. Searching for entities obliged to publish (meta)data to the NAP and providing guidance and help.<br/>Reporting to the MS / NB about its operation. Publishing (meta)data of the Content Producers and their content free-to-all, barrier-free at the NAP. Providing access to CP to update/insert any information about their content or their organization. Handling the exceptions about metadata/data not meeting threshold conditions for its publication (i.e. content is not in prescribed format). Promote the NAP and provide support for the Content Producers and Content Providers. Monitoring of the data quality and usage and providing this information either at NAP (if in the public interest) to NB or MS. Publish metadata quality guidelines.<br/>Keeping the NAP-published metadata up to date. Provide a mechanism to collect feedback from the Content Consumers (CC) and serve as a proxy for the Content providers (CP).<br/><br/>The tasks of the NAP Operator (or NAP) are NOT data processing, they publish Content Producers (meta)data in one place.<br/><br/><b>Represented by:</b><br/>Usually, a department of the MS Ministry of Transport, a federal state-owned organization, or a research organization. Based on the explicit naming of the entity in the national law.<br/>The organization performing the NAP Operator (NO) role can also perform other roles (i.e. Content Producer and Content Consumer) these roles do not intersect and shall be performed in such a way as to avoid conflict of interest. <br/><br/>
