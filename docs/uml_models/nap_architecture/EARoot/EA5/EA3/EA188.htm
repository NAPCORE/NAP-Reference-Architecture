This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities: <br/><br/>(1) The ability to manage the store of Metadata. <br/>(2) The ability to ensure that all data sent to the store is stored in a coherent and logical manner. <br/>(3) The ability to read, edit, update and erase data from the store as and when requested. <br/>(4) The ability to enable Metadata to be created in advance of their publication on the NAP so it could be checked before its publication. <br/>(5) The ability to carry out its activities in such a way that they do not interfere with one another, and that the integrity of the data being stored and read is preserved. <br/>(6) This function manages all data mentioned in the mobilityDCAT-AP (https://mobilitydcat-ap.github.io/mobilityDCAT-AP/releases/index.html) and other relevant information (e.g. quality info, possible data comments etc.) <br/>
