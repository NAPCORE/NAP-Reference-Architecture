Independent entity performing compliance assessment of the Content Consumers, their specific content, and of NAPs operation and reporting its findings to the MS. <br/><br/><b>Key issue:</b><br/>Provide a compliance assessment of the CPs and NOs with the delegated regulations, and share these assessments with the CP and NO. To operate objectively and independently from the NAP operator (NO) and content producer (CP).<br/><br/><b>Tasks and responsibilities: </b><br/>Randomly and regularly check the compliance of CPs and NOs with the delegated regulations (data and metadata are compliant and processes of sharing the data and reporting are compliant to the rules set up by delegated regulations). To follow up on non-compliant results, to report results to the MS and NO. To collaborate with CP to reach a successful Compliance Assessment.   <br/><br/><b>Represented by:</b><br/>Usually, a department of the MS Ministry of Transport and an independent body. Based on the explicit naming of the entity in the national law.<br/>The NB <u>shall be independent</u> of CPs and NOs since it checks its compliance.<br/><br/>
