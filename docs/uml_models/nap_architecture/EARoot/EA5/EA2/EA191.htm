<b>Parties</b>:  Content Provider (CP) to the NAP operator (NO)<br/><b>Objective</b>: To set up terms under which the content provided to the NAP can be used. Set up obligations and responsibilities of the NAP towards the CP<br/><b>Form</b>: Contract, terms of conditions, license.<br/><b>Obligations</b>:<br/><ul>
<li>CP to NO<ul>
<li>To provide (meta)data under regular terms and conditions including </li><li>To provide proof that the data are compliant with the EU requirements (self-declarations) enabling NAP to publish data.</li><li>To publish data in agreed formats and according to the accepted terms</li><li>To provide the right to reuse the metadata including personal information</li></ul>
</li><br/><li>NO to CP<ul>
<li>To assist in publishing (meta)data to the NAP</li><li>To have a right not to publish or remove published data that do not meet a minimum standard of metadata quality.</li><li>To ascertain that data are not changed in any manner by NAP (NAP can not change data in any way, if it does then not in the NAP role)</li></ul>
</li><br/></ul>
