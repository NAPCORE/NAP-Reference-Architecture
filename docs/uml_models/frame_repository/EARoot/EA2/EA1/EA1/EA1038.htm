This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to manage the content of the store of Service Information Data (D1.3).<br/>(2) The management of the store shall enable the Electronic Payments Operator to review the contents of the store.<br/>(3) The management of the store shall also enable the contents of the store to be updated by Service Providers.<br/>(4) The ability to provide details of services on request to the Create EP Contract function. <br/>

Details:
(a) continuously monitor for receipt of the input data flows from the Electronic Payments Operator, any of the service providers or the Create EP Contract function;<br/>(b) when data flow is received from the Electronic Payments Operator collect all of the current data from the store of Service Information Data (D1.3);<br/>(c) send the response to the Electronic Payments Operator in the service data data flow;<br/>(d) when a data flow in (a) is received from the any of the service providers load its contents into the store of Service Information Data (D1.3);<br/>(e) when the data flow is received from the Create EP Contract function, collect data about the requested services from the store of Service Information Data (D1.3);<br/>(f) send the data collected in (e) back to the Create EP Contract function in the available service data data flow.<br/>
