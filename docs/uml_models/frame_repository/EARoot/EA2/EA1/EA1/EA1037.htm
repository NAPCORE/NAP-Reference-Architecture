This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to process the different contracts made by the Travellers to produce statistical information for the corresponding Electronic Fee Collection Operators, or Service Providers.<br/>(2) The ability to respond to requests from ITS Service providers for statistical reports about the contracts that the Traveller has set up for their services.<br/>(3) The ability to provide a HMI through which the Electronic Payments Operator can request a statistical report for all of the contracts.<br/>
