This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) A HMI through which the Traveller can be provided with a list of the transactions they have performed.<br/>(2) The HMI shall also provide the Traveller with the status of their Electronic Payment account(s).<br/>(3) Also the HMI shall only provide both types of information following a specific request from the Traveller and shall only provide information that is directly relevant to that Traveller.<br/>
