Appears In: Functional Tree of Area F9 Provide Support for Cooperative Systems, DFD F9.5 Manage Use of Loading or Unloading Zones
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to continuously monitor the use of each parking place in a Holding Zone.<br/>(2) The ability for its sensors to detect that a Vehicle is occupying a parking place and to use the data from the sensors to determine the Vehicle identity.<br/>(3) The ability to send the determined identity to the Manage Loading or Unloading Zone Bookings Function. <br/>
