This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to take responsibility for the output of information about road conditions, PT services, conditions of other transport modes, Points of Interest (POI) and Personal Services (PS) to Travellers.<br/>(2) The information output to the Traveller shall be provided by the Travel Information Operator through the Manage Traveller Information Data Store Function.<br/>(3) The ability to output the information in a form that is easy to understand and be suitable for those with disabilities.<br/>(4) The ability to continuously display the particular information that is being output until replaced by other information or the output is cancelled by the Operator.<br/>
