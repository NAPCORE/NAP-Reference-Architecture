Appears In: DFD F5.16 Provide Outputs to Host Vehicle Drivers
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to receive dynamic warnings and instructions about various situations and events that will affect the progress of the Vehicle through the road network.<br/>(2) The ability to filter these dynamic warnings and instructions according to the current Vehicle location.<br/>(3) The ability to send the filtered dynamic warnings and instructions for display to the Driver.<br/>(4) The ability to process warnings that include accidents, incidents, wrong-way drivers, hazardous weather conditions, roadworks, predicted traffic conditions and congestion.<br/>(5) The ability for each item of information sent for output to the Driver to be given a priority level based on such things as their relevance to the location of the Vehicle, the likelihood that the Vehicle will encounter them, and their possible effect on the safety of the Vehicle and its occupants.<br/>(6) The ability to filter the warnings and instructions so that only those that are appropriate and relevant to the current location of the Vehicle are sent for output to the Driver.<br/>

(a) continuously monitor for receipt of any of the input data flows;<br/>(b) when in (a) a new traffic regulation, lane command, or either of the queue information data flows are received, it shall be filtered according to the current location of the Vehicle determined in (i) below;<br/>(c) the resulting filtered data shall be passed on to the Output Commands and Dynamic Warnings function for display to the Driver using the content for driver output data flow;<br/>(d) the information shall also be sent in the information to other vehicles data flow to the Provide Vehicle to Vehicle Communications function so that it can be forwarded to Other Vehicle;<br/>(e) whenever a new dynamic warning is received, it shall be passed on for display to the Driver at the pre-coded location using the content for driver output data flow which is sent to the Output Commands and Dynamic Warnings function;<br/>(f) whenever a new lane instruction is received, it is passed on for display to the Driver at the pre-coded location;<br/>(g) when the data flow containing location data is received in (a), calculate the current location of the Vehicle and store it internally for use within the function;<br/>(h) all data that is received shall be checked against the location of the Vehicle determined from the data provided in (f) to ensure that only the data that is appropriate and relevant to the Vehicle and its direction of travel is sent for output to the Driver by the Provide Dynamic Warnings and Lane Instructions function.<br/>
