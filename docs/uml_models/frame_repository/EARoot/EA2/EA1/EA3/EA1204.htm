This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to manage the output of instructions from an incident strategy to other functionality in the System in  response to incidents that have been detected and classified by other functionality .<br/>(2) The ability for the instructions included in the incident management strategies to be output to require the replacement of, or changes to, any traffic management strategies that are currently in operation.<br/>(3) The ability for the output of the incident management strategies to begin as soon as the strategy information is received.<br/>(4) The ability to keep a local store of the strategies currently being implemented and delete them when their expiry time has passed, or when a strategy modification or removal indication arrives from the incident management functionality.<br/>
