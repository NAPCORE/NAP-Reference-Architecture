This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to detect vehicles using the entrances and exits of car parks in the urban road network.<br/>(2) The ability to collect the data as raw input by sensors that are capable of detecting the passage and presence of all types of road vehicle, from bicycles to Heavy Goods Vehicles (HGV's).<br/>(3) The ability to process the data to provide actual vehicle count data, i.e. numbers of vehicles, using the entrances and exits of each car park.<br/>(4) The ability to send the processed data to other functionality for collation and use in traffic management.<br/>
