This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to provide traffic control facilities that enable the traffic to be managed so that the most efficient use is made of the inter-urban road network.<br/>(2) The ability to manage the implementation of traffic management strategies for the inter-urban road network in a planned sequence according to the time of day and day of week.<br/>(3) The ability for the traffic management strategies to include control of access to the inter-urban network (ramp metering), plus commands to manage the use of lanes in the carriageway (including the hard shoulder) and the maximum speeds for vehicles in each lane.<br/>(4) The ability for these strategies to be overridden by the Road Network Operator through the functionality providing their interface, as well as by inputs from the incident, demand and access management functionality.<br/>(5) The ability to use current, historic and predicted traffic data from the inter-urban network and to change in real-time the actual traffic management commands being sent for output to take account of variations in this data.<br/>(6) The ability to provide details of the current and previous implemented traffic management strategies on some or all parts of the inter-urban road network to the Road Network Operator through the functionality that provides their interface.<br/>
