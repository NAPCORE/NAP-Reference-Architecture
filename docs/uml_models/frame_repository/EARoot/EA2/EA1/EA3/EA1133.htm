Appears In: DFD F3.1.8 Provide Traffic Management for Bridges, Functional Tree of F3.1 Provide Traffic Control
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to manage the traffic using one or more bridges that are located on the road network served by the System.<br/>(2) The ability to monitor one or more bridges that are located on the road network served by the System. <br/>(3) The ability to collect weather and other conditions on the bridge(s) that it is managing using its own sensors and receive weather forecasts for the local geographic area(s) around the bridge(s).<br/>(4) The ability to automatically initiate action when conditions likely to endanger the traffic using the bridge(s) are detected, using actions that have been previously defined by the Bridge Operator, including the output of messages to Drivers either directly on the bridge or through other roadside equipment.<br/>(5) If no action is defined, the ability to use the Bridge Operator HMI provided by other functionality to present the Operator with options for actions to be taken that must either be accepted or amended before they are implemented.<br/>(6) The ability to send both the actions taken and details about the dangerous conditions as an incident to the relevant part of other functionality in the System.<br/>(7) The ability to also receive the details of the content of messages informing Drivers about traffic management strategies that it should output to Drivers using the bridges(s) that it is managing in the absence of any higher priority messages of its own once confirmation is received from the Bridge Operator.<br/>(8) If any faults are detected by its sensors or other equipment, the ability to report this to the appropriate functionality in the System.<br/>
