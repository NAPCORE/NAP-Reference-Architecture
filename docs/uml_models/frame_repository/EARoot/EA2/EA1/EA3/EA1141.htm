Appears In: DFD F3.1.1.5 Provide Urban Traffic Management Facilities, Functional Tree of F3.1.1 Provide Traffic Control
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to provide the management of vehicle speed and headway settings within the urban road network.<br/>(2) The ability to receive commands to implement legal speed settings, plus both suggested speed and headway settings from either the functionality providing the Road Network Operator interface, or the urban traffic management functionality, or as part of an incident, demand management, or environmental strategy.<br/>(3) The ability to ensure that requests from the functionality providing the Road Network Operator interface take priority and override those from the urban traffic management functionality, but not those that are part of an incident, demand management, or environmental strategy.<br/>(4) The ability to send speed and headway settings to the functionality that is responsible for the output of messages to Drivers of vehicles using the urban road network, both at the roadside and in the Vehicle, as well as to other functionality from which it can be sent to other parts of the system, to the Broadcaster entity.<br/>(5) The ability to send the legal speed settings to the digital map data provider entity in case it needs to be used in future digital map updates.<br/>

(a) continuously monitor for receipt of the input data flows;<br/>(b) when the urban road legal speeds data flow is received in (a) store its contents locally for future use;<br/>(c) as a result of (b) send the new urban speed limits in the urban legal speed limits data flow to the Provide Support for Host Vehicle Systems functionality;<br/>(d) when either the urban speed and headway settings or the operator urban speed override data flows are received in (a) implement the speed that they contain by sending the new speed limit in the urban speed limit changes and updated urban speed limits data flows;<br/>(e) in (d) the speed limit in the operator urban speed override shall take precedence over the value in the urban speed setting data flow, except where this is provided as part of an incident, demand or environmental management strategy;<br/>(f) as a result of (d) and (e) also send the new speed limit and headway to the Provide Support for Host Vehicle Systems functionality in the urban suggested speeds and headways data flow and to the Output Lane & Speed Commands to Urban Roads function in the urban speed l&s commands data flow.<br/>
