This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to continuously monitor the area around the entrance to a rest area in a service area for the approach of Vehicles.<br/>(2) When its sensors detect that a Vehicle is approaching the ability to use the data from the sensors to determine the Vehicle identity.<br/>(3) The ability to send the Vehicle identity data to the functionality that manages service area rest area bookings.<br/>
