This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to provide output of information about car parks to Vehicle Drivers, which shall be updated as soon as new data is received.<br/>(2) The ability to output information about relevant service areas, if it is available from other functionality.<br/>(3) The ability for the output to show either the current car park occupancy (number of spaces) or the current status depending on what is required by the Parking Operator.<br/>
