This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to receive from the functionality managing the store of Traffic Simulation Data the results of a simulation that have been produced by the Traffic Simulation Engine functionality.<br/>(2) The ability to process these results to provide coherent and comprehensive information about forecasts of traffic conditions and traffic management strategies.<br/>(3) The ability to automatically send this information to the appropriate functionality in the System.<br/>
