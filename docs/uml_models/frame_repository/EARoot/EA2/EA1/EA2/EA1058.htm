This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) An appropriate link to communicate to the Emergency Services the notification that a Vehicle has been stolen has been received.<br/>(2) The ability for the link to provide some form of alarm indication to draw the attention of the Emergency Services to the fact that a Vehicle has been stolen.<br/>(3) An appropriate link to communicate to the Emergency Services the notification that the stolen notification for a Vehicle has been cancelled, with again some form of alarm indication to draw the attention to the new status.<br/>
