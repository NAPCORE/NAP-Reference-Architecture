This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to manage the contents of the Store of Incident and Emergency Data.<br/>(2) The ability to receive data about incidents and the way that their responses are being processed (emergency plans) and update them in the Store of Incident and Emergency Data.<br/>(3) The ability to pass on incident descriptions when they are received to the functionality for planning emergency interventions.<br/>
