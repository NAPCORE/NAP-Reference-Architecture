This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to collect incident notifications from a variety of sources.<br/>(2) The capability to filter and obtain associated information (e.g. location, cargo status, Vehicle identification, Traveller identification) to produce the data needed for the planning of the appropriate response from the Emergency Services.<br/>(3) The ability to provide an acknowledgement of incident notification to its source, e.g. from a Pedestrian and to follow this up with reports of progress with providing an appropriate response.<br/>(4) The ability to respond in a timely manner to requests for status updates from the eCall Message Management functionality<br/>(5) The ability to respond instantly to the receipt of eCall messages.<br/>(6) The ability to classify incidents and to provide data about them to other functionality so that the appropriate response can be planned and implemented and the appropriate traffic management action can be taken.<br/>

Details:
(a) continuously monitor for the receipt of PSAP status request data flows;<br/>(b) when the data flow in (a) is received, instantly respond with the current status;<br/>(c) continuously monitor for receipt of data flows containing eCall messages and incident notifications from other sources, e.g. Pedestrians;<br/>(d) when a data flow in (c) is received, take appropriate action immediately including sending acknowledgement to the source;<br/>(e) take immediate action to respond to the incident including the initiation of action by the Emergency Services and appropriate traffic management measures;<br/>(f) send a request store data objects about the incident to the appropriate functionality;<br/>(g) review the data provided about the incident and associate with it a confidence parameter;<br/>(h) gather any further available data about the incident and increase associate confidence parameter as this is collected and stored;<br/>(i) when the confidence that an incident has occurred is right create an emergency;<br/>(j) complete the collection of information on the Vehicle(s) and/or Pedestrians who have been involved in the incident;<br/>(h) send a request store data objects about the emergency to the appropriate functionality;<br/>(i) send a request to the appropriate functionality that planning of the incident response is initiated.<br/>
