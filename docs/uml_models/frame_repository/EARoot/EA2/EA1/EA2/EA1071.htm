Appears In: Functional Tree of Area F2 Provide Safety and Emergency Facilities, DFD F2.1.2 Manage Emergency Intervention
This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:<br/><br/>(1) The ability to provide the full acknowledgement of the response to incidents to the originators.<br/>(2) The ability to prepare reports about the current progress with the response to incidents, i.e. how the emergency plan is being implemented.<br/>(3) The ability to provide updates to the functionality that provides the management of road traffic in the geographic area where the incident occurred.<br/>
