Appears In: DFD F4.7 Provide On-Demand Services
This Data Store shall be used within the Manage Public Transport Operations Functional Area.  It shall contain information objects that provide a record of the delivery of On-Demand Services.  As a minimum the information objects can be divided into two parts: about the Vehicles used to provide the On-Demand Services and a record of previous services.<br/><br/>Each information object about the Vehicles used to provide the On-Demand Services in the Store shall contain the following data objects:<br/>(1) Time (numbers defining hours, minutes and seconds),<br/>(2) Date (date string),<br/>(3) Vehicle ID (number),<br/>(4) Vehicle type (characters),<br/>(5) Current status (characters),<br/>(6) Current location (characters and/or numbers, e.g. GPS/Galileo data),<br/>(7) ID of Service currently being delivered (number).<br/><br/>The information objects in the Store that provide a record of previous services shall contain the following data objects:<br/>(8) Service ID (number),<br/>(9) Origin (characters and/or numbers, e.g. GPS/Galileo data),<br/>(10) Destination (characters and/or numbers, e.g. GPS/Galileo data),<br/>(11) Route (data for a digital roadmap using a standard format, e.g. GDF, plus characters and/or numbers, e.g. GPS/Galileo data),<br/>(12) Date of delivery (date string),<br/>(13) Time of delivery (numbers defining hours and minutes),<br/>(14) Performance, e.g. stops at which on-time, early or late (text/numbers).<br/><br/>It shall be possible for the Vehicle data to be accumulated for each Vehicle to provide a historic record of its use.  Also it shall be possible for the record of previous Services data to be maintained in perpetuity to provide a source of information for the On-demand Service Operator.<br/>
