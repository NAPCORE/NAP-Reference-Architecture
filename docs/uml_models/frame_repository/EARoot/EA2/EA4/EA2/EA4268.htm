This Terminator is part of the Vehicle Environment Terminator and shall represent the manifestation of other road users.  These objects may be other Vehicles, motor-cyclists, cyclists, animals, pedestrians, Vulnerable Road Users (VRU's), etc. that are in the roadway in the immediate geographic area surrounding the Vehicle.  It is expected that this Terminator shall provide manifestations of other road users in such a way that they can be detected by sensors which shall create data objects for use by the functionality in the system to enable the Vehicle navigate through the road network.  The functionality in the system shall identify the Vehicle in which is it operating as the Host Vehicle.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: ve.oru<br/>
