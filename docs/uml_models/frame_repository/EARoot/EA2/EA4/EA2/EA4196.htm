This Terminator shall represent weather effects such as snow, rain and fog, pollution effects such as dust and smoke, and man-made electromagnetic effects through which ITS Services are to be delivered.  It is a physical entity from which data objects can be obtained through functionality that analyses the output of sensors capable of detecting the weather and other effects.  The data objects produced by this functionality shall be used for a variety of purposes connected with the operation and maintenance of ITS Services and may therefore influence road traffic management strategies plus the choices Travellers make in the planning of their trips.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: ae<br/>
