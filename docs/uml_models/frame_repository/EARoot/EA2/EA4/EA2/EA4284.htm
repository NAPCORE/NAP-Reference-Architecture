This Generic Terminator Role Content Provider shall represent any provider of traffic, travel data. <br/>The Content Provider describes this data with metadata on the NAP (to be stored in D3.15 NAP Metadata). The Content Provider must be registered in the NAP, the NAP Content Provider electronic identity, proof of real-world identity and authorization credentials are kept in D3.17 NAP Users. The Content Provider information is encompassing one or more user accounts and machine accounts, (for Data Platform NAP type), licence agreements and other relevant data based on the provider complexity. The Content Provider is always linked with at least one user account that also stands for the organization.<br/>In case of Data Platform NAP type the Content Provider also uploads / make data available for retrieval by the NAP.<br/>Content Providers are for example public and private road operators, road authorities, service providers, transport authorities, transport operators, infrastructure managers, transport on-demand service providers. <br/>Obligations to make data content accessible via the NAP is stipulated in the ITS Directive 2010/40/EU and its revised version (EU) 2023/2661 and complementary Delegated Regulations 885/2013, 886/2013, 2015/962, 2017/1926, 2022/670 and 2023/1804,<br/><b>Key issue</b>: Provide and publish content (to which he has the legal rights to publish); collect content. <br/><b>Area of responsibility</b>: Provide timely and accurate traffic and travel data.<br/><b>Tasks</b>: Provide content relevant to the ITS domain and according to contracts with the NAP and content consumers. provide information for issuing electronic identity, proving of real-world identity, issuing authentication credentials to prove themselves as trusted and registered NAP Content provider.<br/><b>Mnemonic</b>: nap.cp<br/><br/>===========<br/><b>CHANGELOG:</b><br/>- added information about the user and machine account as being part of the CP<br/>- added information about the organization account and user account.<br/>- added information about possibility to upload data to NAP<br/>- added task description.<br/><b>TODO:</b><br/>- check data flows so they contain the correct information<br/>- add information about new delegated regulations, possibly directly into the CP description.<br/><br/>
