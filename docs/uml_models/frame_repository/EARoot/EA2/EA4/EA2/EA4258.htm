This Terminator is part of the External Service Provider Terminator and shall represent an organiser of external events, such as football matches, parades, music festivals, etc. that are likely to attract a large number of attendees, the presence of which may have an impact on the travel conditions on the surrounding road network.  It is expected that this Terminator shall at various times provide data objects containing information about these events such as, time, place, duration, expected numbers of attendees, etc. for use by the incident management functionality in the system.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: esp.peo<br/>
