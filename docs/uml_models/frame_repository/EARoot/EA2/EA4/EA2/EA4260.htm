This Terminator is part of the Vehicle Terminator and shall represent a Vehicle that is designed and licensed for the purpose of carrying freight of any kind.  This type of Vehicle shall include those with and without trailers or "swap bodies", but not include light vans as these are classed as ordinary vehicles.  It is expected that this Terminator shall provide data objects to the functionality in the system either directly through an interface or through sensors, and these data objects shall be used to assist in providing the ITS Services.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: v.fv<br/>
