This Terminator is part of the Vehicle Terminator and shall represent devices within the Vehicle that shall interact directly with the Driver or other occupants to enable the eCall ITS Service to be used and to providing traffic information in the Vehicle.  It is expected that this Terminator shall provide data objects to the functionality in the system either directly through an interface or through sensors, and these data objects shall be used to provide the eCall Service.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: v.hmi<br/>
