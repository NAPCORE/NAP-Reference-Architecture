This Terminator is part of the Vehicle Terminator and shall represent a Vehicle that is licensed to use the road network, carry passengers who pay for their transportation and who expect it to follow a fixed or predicted route to provide a Public Transport (PT) service.  It is expected that this Terminator shall provide data objects to the functionality in the system either directly through an interface or through sensors, and these shall be used to assist in providing the PT services.  In addition, the Terminator shall be sent data objects through an interface by functionality in the system that requests the output of instructions for the PT Driver to follow.  Note that this shall be a "request" and not a command, as it shall not be possible for the PT Vehicle to be controlled by the functionality in the system.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: v.ptv<br/>
