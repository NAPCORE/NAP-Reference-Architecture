This Terminator is part of the Maintenance Organisation Terminator and shall represent an organisation that is able to carry out maintenance of Public Transport (PT) related equipment that is used to provide the ITS Services for which functionality exists within the system.  It shall be possible for the Terminator to both receive data objects from functionality in the system about PT related equipment maintenance that is required and provide data objects showing the progress with and/or plans for that maintenance.  The functionality in the system must also be able to communicate with more than one Public Transport Maintenance Organisation.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: mo.ptmo<br/>
