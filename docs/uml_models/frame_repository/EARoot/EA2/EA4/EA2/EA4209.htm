This Terminator shall represent other instances of different functionality that is included in parts of the system. Each different instance shall be represented by the following Terminators:<br/><br/>Emergency Management System<br/>Environmental Traffic Management System<br/>Hazardous Goods Vehicle Route Monitoring<br/>Incident Traffic Management System<br/>Inter-urban Traffic Management System<br/>Other Navigation Device<br/>Public Transport Management System<br/>Public Transport Stop<br/>Traffic Signal Controller<br/>Traffic Simulation System<br/>Urban Traffic Management System<br/><br/>These Terminators shall enable the same data objects to be sent to and received from other instances of the functionality that they each represent.  These other instances may serve other geographic areas, or be managed and operated by different organisations serving the same geographic area.  It shall be possible for the data objects to be sent and received on request, at regular intervals, or because the sending functionality has made changes.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: ors<br/><br/>
