This Terminator is part of the Freight Transport Terminator and shall represent a human entity that manages and represents the Freight Transport Service Provider.  It shall be possible for this Terminator to negotiate contracts to provide freight transport in response to requests from the Freight Transport User, who is the customer.  The provision of the freight transport shall be manifest through a Freight Transport Execution Plan, which is prepared by the system in response to a request from the Freight Transport Operations Manager to fulfil the demands of the Freight Transport User.  The preparation process shall be managed by the Freight Transport Operations Manager and once created the Freight Transport Execution Plan must be approved by the Freight Transport User before being implemented.  The implementation of the Freight Transport Execution Plan shall be managed and monitored by the Freight Transport Operations Manager, who if necessary shall be able to modify a Plan during its implementation.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: ft.ftom<br/>
