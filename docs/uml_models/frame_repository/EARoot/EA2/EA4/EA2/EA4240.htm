This Terminator is part of the Bridge/Tunnel Infrastructure Terminator and shall represent the outputs provided by tunnel management systems that monitor the operation of equipment in a tunnel.  The outputs shall provide data objects that show such things as the state of the atmosphere inside the tunnel (pollution detection), if a fire or other hazardous condition has been detected, or if the systems themselves are operating correctly.  These data objects shall be used by functionality to manage the flow of road traffic entering the tunnels to which they relate.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: bti.tms<br/>
