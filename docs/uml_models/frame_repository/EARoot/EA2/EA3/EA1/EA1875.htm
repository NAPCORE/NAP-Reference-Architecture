It contains data objects that provide information being sent from Vehicles to functionality in the Manage Traffic Functional Area, e.g. floating car data and Vehicles that have become moving incidents, i.e. they have not been involved in accidents or are broken down and are moving through the road network either continuously, e.g. wide/long load, or from time to time, e.g. road/winter maintenance vehicles.<br/>
