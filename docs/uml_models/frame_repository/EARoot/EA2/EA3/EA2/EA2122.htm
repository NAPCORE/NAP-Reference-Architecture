Appears In: DFD F1.6 Manage Tariffs and Access Rights
It contains data objects provided by the General Information Provider Terminator which is part of the External Services Provider Terminator for use by functionality in the Provide Electronic Payment Facilities Functional Area.  The information objects are used to define the criteria to be applied to enable the determination of the rights of a user to use a given service and comprise the following:<br/>- service provider ID,<br/>- service ID,<br/>- period of validity of criteria,<br/>-  list of criteria, which itself comprises:<br/>     - type of contract,<br/>     - period of day or year,<br/>     -level of sensitivity of information.<br/>
