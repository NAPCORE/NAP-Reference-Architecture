It consists of inputs from the Multi-Modal Crossing containing data objects that represent warning messages for output to Drivers, Cyclists and Pedestrians not to enter a multi-modal crossing because either it is about to closed to road traffic, or has closed but is a short distance ahead, and will stop or restrict the normal flow of road traffic throughout the urban road network.<br/>
