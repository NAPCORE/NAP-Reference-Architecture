It contains data objects that provide information about predicted unusual traffic conditions within the total road network using the results from traffic simulations and is for use in the monitoring of a trip being implemented by a Traveller using a nomadic device. The causes of these unusual traffic conditions may include such things as expected road works and road blocks. In both cases the predicted travel times may exceed normal times.<br/>
