It contains data objects that provide the recommendations established by functionality in the Manage Traffic Functional Area for the enforcement of rules in the urban road network.  The data flow includes the following data objects:<br/>- date<br/>- period of validity of guidelines<br/>- area concerned<br/>- types of violation concerned<br/>- for each type of violation: level of violation over which notification and / or prosecution is required, according to environmental conditions (weather, traffic conditions, etc.).<br/>
