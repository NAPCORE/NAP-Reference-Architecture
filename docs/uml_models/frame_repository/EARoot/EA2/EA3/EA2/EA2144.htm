It contains data objects provided by the Traffic and Travel Information Provider Terminator which is part of the External Services Provider Terminator for use by functionality in the Provide Electronic Payment Facilities Functional Area.  The information objects define each ITS Service and its method of payment that is being offered to the users using the following elements:<br/>- ITS Service ID,<br/>- nature of ITS Service,<br/>- ID of the ITS Service provider,<br/>- associated account (where the payment will go),<br/>- location(s) of the ITS Service (where the user can use it),<br/>- types of contracts possible,<br/>- categories of people allowed to use this ITS Service (that is to pass a contract for it, regardless of access rights which are defined by regulating bodies),<br/>- enforcement procedures,<br/>- modes of booking,<br/>- identification of tariffs,<br/>- rules of fee apportionment if several providers provide the same ITS Service,<br/>-list of the ID's of ITS Services grouped for the apportionment.<br/>Note: if an ITS Service is provided at no cost to the user, this data flow will not be used.<br/><br/>
