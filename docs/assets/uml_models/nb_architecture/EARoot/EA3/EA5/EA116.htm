Management of the list of stakeholders<br/>Requirements: The list of respondents is formed on the basis of the state register (e.g. register of legal units, business register, etc.) and is constantly updated. The following data should be stored in the list: company name, address, contact information, ID of self-declaration form (Template), reporting period, etc.<br/>Create a list of respondents to whom the request for information will be sent  Requirements: Based on common criteria (agreed within NAPCORE) a list of respondents will be created. Respondents from the list will be requested to provide information/documents for the compliance with the requirements set out in the delegated act.<br/>
