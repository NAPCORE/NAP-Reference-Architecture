<b>Title</b>: Open Data Directive<br/><b>Reference</b>: Directive (EU) 2019/1024<br/><b>Type</b>: Legal Specification<br/><b>Full Name and Hyperlink</b>: <a href="file://https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/dir/2019/1024/oj/eng \t _blank"><font color="#467886"><u>Directive (EU) 2019/1024 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 June 2019</u></font></a><br/><b>Contextual Use</b>: Establishes a framework for the reuse of public sector information, promoting the availability of open data. Aims to boost the socioeconomic potential of public sector information by making it more accessible for startups, SMEs, and other entities.
