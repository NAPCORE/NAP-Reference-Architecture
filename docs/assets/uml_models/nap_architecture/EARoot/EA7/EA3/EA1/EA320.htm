<b>“Support of commonly used languages”</b> KPI may take four possible values. A value equal to 0 indicates that a NAP supports only the national language. A value equal to 1 indicates that although a NAP supports English, some of its content (e.g., metadata of included publications) is provided only in the national language. On the other hand, a value equal to 2 indicates that a NAP fully supports the English language.<br/><br/>Note: This is relevant to the webpage while this is not relevant to the metadata/data content that depend on its publisher.<br/><br/>0: Supports national language only, other than EN<br/>1: Supports EN but some content in national language only (other than EN)<br/>2: Supports EN for platform and all content (user interface and all text content)<br/><br/>Minimum acceptable value for interoperability: 1<br/>
