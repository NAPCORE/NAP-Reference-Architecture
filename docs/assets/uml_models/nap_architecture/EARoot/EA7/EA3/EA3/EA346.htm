<b>Search results” </b>KPI aims to assess the performance of a NAP in terms of presenting and further analyzing search results. Foreseen possibilities include the listing of search results, their further filtering and sorting, or even their visualization (e.g., in the form of a map). It is assumed that a simple listing of search results constitutes a minimum acceptable operational prerequisite of a NAP.<br/><br/>Are the search results displayed?<br/><br/>0: No<br/>1: Yes (at least list of search results)<br/>Value+1 (for each of the following options): Are there more features than a basic list of search results? How are the search results displayed? You can select more options:<br/>a. List of search results that can be further “filtered or sorted”<br/>b. Map-based presentation of search results<br/>c. Option to save search pattern or settings<br/><br/>Minimum acceptable value for interoperability: 1<br/>
