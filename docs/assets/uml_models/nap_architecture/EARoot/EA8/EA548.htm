This Generic Actor Role Content Provider shall represent any provider of traffic and transport data.<br/><br/>The Content Provider describes this data with meta-data on the NAP (to be stored in D3.15 NAP Meta-Data). Information about the Content Provider, such as contact details, shall be kept in D3.17 NAP Content Providers.<br/>Content Providers are for example public and private road operators, road authorities, service providers, transport authorities, transport operators, infrastructure managers, transport on-demand service providers. The obligations to make contents accessible via the NAP mainly depends on the type of data according to the Delegated Regulations 885/2013, 886/2013, 962/2015, 1926/2017<br/><br/><b>Key issue</b>: Provide and publish content (to which he has the legal rights to publish); collect content <br/><br/><b>Area of responsibility</b>: Provide timely and accurate traffic data<br/><br/><b>Tasks</b>: Provide content relevant to the ITS domain and according to contracts with the NAP and content consumers<br/><br/>Mnemonic: nap.cp<br/><br/>
