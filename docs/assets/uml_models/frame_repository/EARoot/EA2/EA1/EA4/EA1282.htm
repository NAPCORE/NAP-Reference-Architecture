This High-level Function shall provide operation management functionality by managing drivers, crew and vehicles. It shall cover the provision of on-demand transport services to individual travellers and car sharing services. The Function shall continuously monitor the status of the infrastructure and vehicles and then deal with routine and non-routine maintenance.<br/>
