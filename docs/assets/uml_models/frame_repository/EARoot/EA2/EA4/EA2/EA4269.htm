This Terminator is part of the Vehicle Environment Terminator and shall represent the manifestation of stationary objects.  These objects may be anything such as solid objects, e.g. debris, that are in or near the roadway of the immediate geographic area that surrounds the Vehicle.  It is expected that this Terminator shall provide manifestations of stationary objects in such a way that they can be detected by sensors which shall create data objects for use by the functionality in the system to enable the Vehicle navigate through the road network.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: ve.so<br/>
