This Terminator is part of the Maintenance Organisation Terminator and shall represent an organisation that is able to carry out work to maintain the road network and any equipment that is used to manage traffic using the road network.  It shall be possible for this Terminator to be sent requests for maintenance work to be performed on the road network and/or any of the equipment.  The Terminator is also expected to provide data objects to functionality in the system about the time, place and duration of planned road works and the status of previously requested maintenance work.  The maintenance work is expected to include any repairs to the physical road network such as worn surfaces and faulty equipment, plus the management of operations and use of de-icing and snow clearing equipment in various parts of the road network.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: mo.rmo<br/>
