This Terminator shall represent the material that makes up the surface of the road. It shall be possible for the functionality in the system to use its sensors to create data objects that enable it to identify the status of the road and/or decide what maintenance operations are necessary to ensure that the surface causes no hazard to vehicles, pedestrians, as well as to those in wheelchairs, or have imperfect sight.  The different status of the road surfacing that can be measured shall include but not be limited to conditions such as rough or warn surfaces, ice, flood water, landslides, etc.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: rp<br/>
