This Terminator is part of the Other Related System Terminator and shall represent another instance of the functionality in the system that can be used to simulate traffic flow in the road network.  It shall enable the same data objects to be sent to and received from other instances of the traffic flow simulation functionality.  These other instances may belong to another road traffic management organisation operating in the same geographic area, or to a road traffic management organisation operating in a different geographic area.  It shall be possible for the data objects to be sent and received on request, at regular intervals, or because the sending functionality has made changes.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: ors.tss<br/>
