It consists of inputs from the Law Enforcement Agency containing data objects that describe an update of the rules that must be followed by Vehicles and users of the road transport network.  They are for use by  functionality in the Provide Support for Law Enforcement Functional Area.  The description of the update includes: date, domain concerned, list of new rules applicable and list of rules updated.<br/>
