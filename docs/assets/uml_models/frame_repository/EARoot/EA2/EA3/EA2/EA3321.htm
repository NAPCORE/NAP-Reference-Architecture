Appears In: DFD F5.14 Provide In-vehicle Trip Planning & Implementation, DFD F9.5 Manage Use of Loading or Unloading Zones
It contains data objects that provide a rejection of the previous booking for a parking space in a holding zone because the Expected Time of Arrival (ETA) of the Freight Vehicle for which the booking was made has changed.  The data objects include alternative times when a suitable parking space will be available and/or the availability of another holding area where the Freight Vehicle can wait for a space in the requested loading or unloading zone to become available.<br/>
