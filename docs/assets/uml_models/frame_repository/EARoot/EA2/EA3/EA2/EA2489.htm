It contains inputs from the Weather Systems from which data objects are created that provide details of the forecast of weather conditions across the road network.  These data objects shall comprise but not be limited to:<br/>- date(s) for which the weather forecast applies,<br/>- for every hour during which the forecast is valid:<br/>    - predicted wind speed and direction,<br/>    - predicted maximum/minimum temperatures,<br/>    - percentage chance of rain/snow/ice,<br/>    - percentage chance of fog,<br/>-  variations in the above details due to geography, e.g. hills and/or mountains and water features such as rivers, lakes and the sea.<br/>
