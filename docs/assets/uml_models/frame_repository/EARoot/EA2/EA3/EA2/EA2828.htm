It contains data objects that provide a rejection of the previous booking for a parking space in the rest area of a service area because the Expected Time of Arrival (ETA) of the Freight Vehicle for which the booking was made has changed.  Details of alternative times when a suitable parking space will be available are provided by the data objects.<br/>
