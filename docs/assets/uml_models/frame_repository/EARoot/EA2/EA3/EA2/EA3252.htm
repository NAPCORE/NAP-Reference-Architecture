It contains data objects that provide all the information about the services being offered to the user.  The data flow contains the following data objects:<br/>- ITS Service ID,<br/>- nature of ITS Service,<br/>- ID of the ITS Service provider,<br/>- associated account (where the payment will go),<br/>- location(s) of the ITS Service (where the user can use it),<br/>- types of contracts possible,<br/>- categories of people allowed to use this ITS Service (that is to pass a contract for it, regardless of access rights which are defined by regulating bodies),<br/>- enforcement procedures,<br/>- modes of booking,<br/>- identification of tariffs,<br/>- rules of fee apportionment if several providers provide the same ITS Service,<br/>-list of the ID's of ITS Services grouped for the apportionment.<br/>Note: if an ITS Service is provided at no cost to the user, this data flow will not be used.<br/><br/>
