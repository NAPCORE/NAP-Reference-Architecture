Appears In: DFD F3.5 Manage Road Maintenance
This Data Store shall be used within the Manage Traffic Functional Area.  It shall contain information objects that provide records of all maintenance actions that have been carried out, plus those that are yet to be completed.<br/><br/>Each information object in the Store shall contain the following data objects:<br/>(1) Equipment identity (number),<br/>(2) Location (characters and/or numbers, e.g. GPS/Galileo data),<br/>(3) Type of equipment (characters),<br/>(4) Type of fault (characters),<br/>(5) Fault description (characters),<br/>(6) Date reported (date string),<br/>(7) Time reported (numbers defining hours, minutes and seconds),<br/>(8) Date of Maintenance Company notification (date string),<br/>(9) Time of Maintenance Company notification (numbers defining hours, minutes and seconds),<br/>(10) Date of fault clearance notification (date string),<br/>(11) Time of fault clearance notification (numbers defining hours, minutes and seconds),<br/>(12) Action taken to rectify fault (characters),<br/>(13) Maintenance Contractor bonus/penalties (characters and numbers - in currency).<br/><br/>These information objects shall cover all types of equipment (those located at the roadside and at central locations) and also the actual road network itself.  In this case the "equipment identity" data object will contain the road name and/or number whilst the "type of equipment" data object will show the type of road.<br/>
